Open Water

Beginner

OPEN WATER (beginner)
Prerequisites
  • 10 years or older
  • Able to swim / medically fit
  • 8h commitment
What you will learn
  • Knowledge Development to understand basic principles of scuba diving
  • Confined Water Dives to learn basic scuba skills
  • Open Water Dives to use your skills and explore
Description
  • In underwater diving, open water is unrestricted water such as a sea, lake or flooded quarries.
